Phone path

Face the window; phone between you and window for soft front light. Portrait mode sparingly. Lock AE on skin highlight.

15-minute plan

  1. Min 0–3: Clear clutter; sheer on window; set white balance cloudy
  2. Min 3–6: Hero face + shoulder (PPV cover)
  3. Min 6–10: Form frames mid-distance; fabric hierarchy (sheet vs skin)
  4. Min 10–13: Detail hands/jewelry/fabric
  5. Min 13–15: Environment wide + one candid laugh

Content batch

1 window setup → 6 free teasers + 6 PPV stills + 1 detail carousel.

Avoid

  • Overhead ceiling-only light (raccoon eyes)
  • Cold blue WB on skin
  • Rushing poses — hold 3 breaths per frame
  • Cluttered backgrounds fighting the subject
