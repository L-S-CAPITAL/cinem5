Diary aesthetic franchise — white wall, hard flash, anti-studio authenticity audiences trust.

Phone path

Use screen/torch only as weak fill; better: cheap mini flash or continuous LED close + high contrast edit. Or screenshot-vibe: hard room light + exposure lock down.

15-minute plan

  1. Min 0–2: White/neutral wall; kill ambient (darker room)
  2. Min 2–4: M mode 1/200 · f/5.6 · ISO 200; test flash power for skin without blinkies
  3. Min 4–9: Hero stare + 3 distance steps (close/medium/lean)
  4. Min 9–12: Detail (jewelry, waistband, texture) with same flash
  5. Min 12–15: Candid laugh + one ‘almost failed’ frame for authenticity

Content batch

1 wall + flash → 10 diary posts across a week (series continuity) + 3 PPV hard-flash sets.

Avoid

  • Bouncing flash (kills the diary hard shadow)
  • ISO 3200 instead of flash power (noisy, soft look)
  • Standing too far (loses confrontational 35mm energy)
  • Apologetic posing — commit to the stare
